Our Story

Born on the Streets of Pakistan

DesiBites was born from a simple idea: bring the bold, unapologetic flavors of Pakistani street food into a convenient, everyday snack. We started in a small kitchen in Karachi, experimenting with spice blends that remind you of home.

Today, DesiBites is enjoyed by thousands of snack lovers across the country. We use only the finest quality potatoes and real desi spice mixes — no artificial shortcuts, just pure flavor.
